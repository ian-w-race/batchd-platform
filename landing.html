How it works

From scan to confirmed
removal in minutes

1
Manufacturer registers products
Products, lot codes, and shipment records logged in the manufacturer portal. Batch'd knows what went where before a recall is ever needed.
2
Staff scan products on shelf
Store staff use the Batch'd scanning app to log products as they go on shelf. AI extracts lot codes from photos automatically.
3
Recall fires instantly
Manufacturer pushes a recall. All connected retailers are notified immediately with shelf impact scores pre-calculated from shipment records.
4
Full audit trail created
Each store acknowledges, confirms removal, and logs disposal. Every action timestamped. Legal documentation generated automatically.
