Where to find lot codes

Lot codes are small inkjet or laser-etched clusters near the best-by date. Look for L, LOT, BATCH, MFG DATE, or just a string of numbers.

Tip for staff: You don't need to identify which characters are "the lot" — just photograph the small inkjet or laser-etched cluster near the best-by date. Batch'd captures the verbatim text and matches it against active recalls automatically.
