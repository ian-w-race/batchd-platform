How to use it
1 Photograph the label — AI identifies the product automatically
2 Capture the lot number — photograph the printed code on the packaging
3 Confirm placement — tap to log the item as placed on shelf
Scan every product before it goes on the shelf — takes about 15 seconds per item.
If a recall alert appears
If the app shows a red recall warning on Step 3, do not place the item on the shelf.

Set it aside and notify your manager immediately. You can still tap to log the item as a recall record.

Your manager can use the History tab to see all placements and generate compliance reports.
